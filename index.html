    Khans of Kazakh Khanate



    Kerei khan
    (1465-1473)

    One of the founders and the first khan of the Kazakh Khanate. The only son of Bolat, the son of Toktakia



    More

    Zhanibek khan
    (1473-1480)

    Founder of the Kazakh Khanate and the dynasty of Kazakh Khans, son of Barak Khan, great-grandson of Uly Khan.



    More

    Kasym khan
    (1511-1521)

    In history, the name of Kasym Khan is directly related to the strengthening and strengthening of the Kazakh Khanate.



    More

    Esim khan
    (1598-1628)

    Esim Khan is the son of Shygai Khan, who previously lived in the Khan's horde of the Kazakh Khanate in Turkestan.



    More

    Tauke khan
    (1672-1715)

    Khan of the younger zhuz since 1652, and since 1672-ruler of the Kazakh Khanate, son of Khan Salkam Zhangir.



    More



    Bis of Kazakh Khanate



    Tole bi

    Tole comes from the kazakh tribe Dulat, ubtribe Zhanys. He was the head biy of the Kazakh senior juz, as well as an author, orator, poet, politician and a public figure. He was born in Jaysan in what is now Shuy district, Jambyl oblast in 1663.

    Kazybek bi

    He was the head biy of the Kazakh middle juz,The boy received the name of Kazybek.At that time the family could not assume that this restless boy would become a famous orator and great diplomat and his advices would be heard by khans and sultans.

    Aiteke bi

    Aiteke Bi was the statesman, one of the three biys made a great contribution fate of the Kazakh people. Baibekuly Aiteke bi (1644-1700) is a statesman and public figure, who participated in the government of the Kazakh khans Tauke, Bolat, Sameke, Abilmambet and Abylai.
